Tracie Adventure Company

Our goal is to provide thrilling and safe white water rafting experiences that are both exciting and unforgettable. We connect people with nature while inspiring a lasting sense of adventure and appreciation for the outdoors. Our team is dedicated to delivering world-class rafting trips that create meaningful and memorable experiences for every guest.

History

Founded in 2014, our company began with a simple passion for adventure and the outdoors. Over the years, we have grown into one of the top-rated rafting companies, thanks to our dedicated team of experienced guides and our commitment to creating unforgettable experiences.
